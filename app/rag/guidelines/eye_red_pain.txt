EMERGENCY:
- Sudden vision loss or severe eye pain.
- Chemical exposure to eyes.
- Eye trauma with bleeding or visible deformity.

URGENT:
- Red eye with light sensitivity.
- Painful eye with blurred vision.
- Discharge from the eye with fever.
- Contact lens wearers with eye pain.

ROUTINE:
- Mild redness from allergies.
- Dry eye symptoms improving with rest.

GENERAL ADVICE:
- Avoid rubbing eyes.
- Flush eyes with clean water if irritated.
- Remove contact lenses during symptoms.

SAFETY DISCLAIMER:
Not medical advice. For severe eye symptoms, seek emergency care.
