EMERGENCY:
- Facial droop.
- Arm weakness.
- Slurred or unclear speech.
- Sudden severe headache with vomiting or confusion.
- Sudden vision loss.
- Sudden inability to walk, severe imbalance, or collapse.

URGENT:
- Transient weakness lasting less than 1 hour (possible TIA).

ROUTINE:
- No routine category. All stroke-like symptoms require evaluation.

GENERAL ADVICE:
- Time is critical. Stroke symptoms are a medical emergency.

SAFETY DISCLAIMER:
Not medical advice. Call emergency services immediately for stroke signs.
